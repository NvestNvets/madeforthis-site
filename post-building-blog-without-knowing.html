Building a Blog Without Knowing What I'm Doing

6 min read

I started this blog with equal parts excitement and confusion.

I learned what "hosting" meant after I bought it. I changed colors fifteen times. I published anyway.

The point is not to know everything before you begin. The point is to begin, then improve.

If you're building something new, give yourself permission to be a beginner in public.
