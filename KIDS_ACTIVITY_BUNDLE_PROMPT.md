# Kids Activity & Coloring Printable Bundle Prompt

We are creating a Kids Activity & Coloring Printable Bundle for the `Made For This` brand.

IMPORTANT:
- This bundle must NOT include orthodontic or dental-related content.
- This is a general kids bundle only.
- A separate themed bundle can be created later if needed.

## Goal

Design a collection of printable pages that are:
- fun but not overly childish
- slightly challenging for ages `3-7`
- gender-neutral
- calm and engaging, not overstimulating
- clean, minimal, and printer-friendly
- black and white for low-ink home printing
- visually consistent across all pages

## Design Style

- simple, clean line art
- doodle-style but intentional
- modern minimal aesthetic
- thick enough lines for easy coloring
- balanced white space
- calm layout, not chaotic

## Branding

- small footer on each page: `Made For This`
- optional small butterfly icon
- consistent margins
- no loud graphics
- no clutter

## Activity Types

### 1. Coloring Pages

Create pages around:
- everyday scenes like playing, reading, family, and bedtime
- calm lifestyle moments
- simple encouraging phrases:
  - `You've got this`
  - `Be kind`
  - `Keep going`
- neutral themes, not overly seasonal

### 2. Mazes

Create easy to medium mazes with themes like:
- help the character get home
- find the toy
- reach the goal

Keep paths clean and easy to follow.

### 3. Trace & Learn

Create:
- alphabet pages `A-Z`
- number pages `1-10`
- simple word tracing pages
- dotted tracing lines
- matching icons for each word or letter

### 4. Matching Activities

Create:
- object to shadow matching
- simple pair matching with everyday items
- line-drawing matching activities

### 5. Cut & Paste Activities

Create:
- cut shapes and match pages
- build simple objects like a house or face
- safe spacing for scissors

### 6. Find & Seek

Create hidden-object pages where kids find `5-10` items in a scene.

Requirements:
- clean layout
- not cluttered
- slightly challenging but fun

### 7. Quiet Time Pages

These are very important.

Create calming, low-stimulation pages with prompts like:
- `Draw your favorite thing`
- `Draw how you feel`

Keep these especially minimal and peaceful.

## Difficulty

- mix easy, medium, and slightly challenging pages
- keep everything appropriate for ages `3-7`
- avoid overwhelming complexity

## Layout Rules

- one activity per page
- clear title at the top
- main activity centered
- footer branding at the bottom
- consistent structure across all pages

## Output

- export each page individually as PDF
- also combine the full bundle into one PDF
- US Letter size `8.5 x 11`
- high resolution and print-ready

## Final Instruction

Do not overdesign.
Do not clutter pages.
Keep the bundle calm, clean, modern, and consistent with the `Made For This` brand.
