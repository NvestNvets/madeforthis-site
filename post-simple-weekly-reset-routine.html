My Simple Weekly Reset Routine

5 min read

My weekly reset is not fancy, but it works.

  • Review calendar and school schedule
  • Pick top three priorities
  • Prep one meal and one snack option
  • Reset one visible room
  • Create a simple plan for Monday morning

Done in under an hour, this routine lowers stress for the whole house.
