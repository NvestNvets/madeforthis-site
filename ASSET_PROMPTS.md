## Brand Direction

- Brand: `Made For This`
- Voice: calm, encouraging, honest, practical, feminine, faith-adjacent without sounding preachy
- Audience: women, moms, busy households, people rebuilding routines, women in transition, practical lifestyle readers
- Visual direction: warm neutral lifestyle, soft editorial desk scenes, simple everyday beauty, clean printable layouts
- Core themes: weekly reset, simple structure, homemaking flow, routines, quiet encouragement, realistic motherhood, rebuilding after overwhelm
- Color palette: cream, warm beige, soft taupe, light gray, charcoal text
